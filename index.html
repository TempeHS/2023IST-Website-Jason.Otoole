Overview

William Shakespeare, an english playwright, poet and actor. Born in April 1564 in Stratford-upon-Avon, England. He is widely regarded as the greatest writer of all time. He was often known as England's national poet or the 'Bard of Avon'. His works consist of many collaberations, 39 plays, 154 sonnets, and multiple narritive poems. His plays have been translated into almost every language and have been performed more than any other playwright. He remains one of the most influential writers of all time as his writings continue to be studied.
